URL Postman

1. POST https://localhost:44333/apiroulette                      create rulette
2. GET https://localhost:44333/apiroulette                       list rulette
3. PUT https://localhost:44333/apiroulette/{{roulete_id}}/open   open rulette   
4. POST https://localhost:44333/apiroulette/{{roulete_id}}/bet   bet
5. PUT https://localhost:44333/apiroulette/{{roulete_id}}/close  close rulette

The port may vary according to the machine.
